How do I place an order?

Simply browse our product categories, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the prompts to complete your purchase.


What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, and UPI.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Delivery times vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected. Typically, orders are delivered within 3-7 business days.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 15 days of delivery. The product must be in its original condition.

How do I request a refund?

To request a refund, contact our customer support team. Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after the returned item is received.

Are the products genuine?

Yes, we guarantee the authenticity of all products listed on TrumpKart.

How do I find the right size?

Please refer to the size chart provided on each product page for accurate sizing information.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach out to our customer support team via email or phone. Visit our Contact Us page for more details.

What are the customer support hours?

Our customer support team is available from 9 AM to 9 PM, Monday to Saturday.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use advanced security measures to protect your personal information and ensure a safe shopping experience.
